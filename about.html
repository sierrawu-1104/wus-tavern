About Me

My name is Andy Wu and I'm currently a Senior at Northwestern University. I've been cooking almost all my life (my mother taught me when I was around 7), and I more recently discovered the art of mixed drinks (junior year of college).

Personally, I have always found that fond memories that don't involve food and drink alongside friends and family are few and far between—as a result, I figured why not create a website to document some of my favorite recipes!

Feel free to reach out with any questions or concerns!
